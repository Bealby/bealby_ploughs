Bealby Forest Cart

In the 70’s I found the Cart in one of the sheds, and being concerned that it might deteriate, agreed with my uncle that I should look after it. It has two 6ft wooden spoked wheels together with the original chain to haul the tree logs out of the forest.... See more

My father told me that it was made in the 1890’s by two of the workshop plough makers . They had a competition to see who made the best wheel! I was told the name of each person but have forgotten. See less
