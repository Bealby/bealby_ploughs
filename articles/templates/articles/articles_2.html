Interview with Frank Hurst

Highlights Magazine
Summer 2023

Frank Hurst spent thirty-six years as a criminal investigator for the British Government. After leaving the service of the Crown, Frank has devoted his time to writing novels. His Golden Triangle trilogy, set in the Far East, has received wide acclaim and won literary awards. Here he tells us a little more about the second book in the trilogy.
